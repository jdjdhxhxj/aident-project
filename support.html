Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats does StudyMind support?
StudyMind supports a wide range of formats including PDF, DOCX, PPTX, DOC, PPT, and image files (JPG, PNG, HEIC). You can also paste text directly or capture notes with your camera.
How does the AI summarization work?
Our AI analyzes your uploaded materials and extracts key concepts, definitions, and important points. It creates structured summaries, flashcards, and study guides tailored to your learning goals.
Is my data secure and private?
Absolutely. All your files are encrypted end-to-end, and we never share your data with third parties. You can delete your materials at any time, and they'll be permanently removed from our servers.
Can I use StudyMind offline?
Yes! Once your materials are processed, you can access summaries, flashcards, and study plans offline. New uploads and AI features require an internet connection.
How do I create a study plan?
After uploading your materials, select "Create study plan" from the goal options. Tell the AI your exam date or deadline, and it will generate a personalized schedule with daily tasks and milestones.
